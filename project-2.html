When is the first track coming up ?

Coming in
Summer 2025

I am currently developing the first official track for Phil Klab, laying the groundwork for what's to come. This is just the beginning of an exciting sonic journey! Throughout summer and fall 2025, I'll be releasing a series of singles that showcase the unique fusion of melodic trance, funk house, and electro-blues that defines my sound.

These releases will build toward my first complete album 'Triangulation,' set to launch in spring 2026. Each track offers a glimpse into different facets of my musical universe, where complex melodies intertwine with tribal and funk rhythms.
