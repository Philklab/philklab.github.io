Hello I am
Phil Klab

Founded in 2024, Phil Klab (a.k.a. Philippe Labonté) emerged as a unique Electronic performance project in Montreal. My first intention was to build a live electro improvisation setup that would let me create music on the spot — blending melodic and hard trance, funk house, and experimental pop, focusing on what feels right in the moment.
