My Music and Performances

I share live performances and studio experiments on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook when they're worth preserving. These platforms are your direct line to my musical world and the first place I announce upcoming tracks and performances.

My work flows in two main currents: spontaneous live improvisation sets created in the moment with hardware synths and loop stations, and structured sequenced compositions for my upcoming album 'Triangulation' and future live shows.

In the coming months, expect selected track releases and live performance recordings that capture the essence of Phil Klab's electronic improvisations. These recordings will showcase the unique sonic landscapes and experimental approach that define the Phil Klab sound.

Melodic Rebellion: The Phil Klab Incident

After dedicating my life to understanding complex harmonies and sophisticated composition techniques, I now make strange bleeps and bloops with expensive equipment.

Phil Klab is what happens when you give a jazz nerd too many synthesizers and not enough supervision. Curious how someone goes from playing jazz fusion to performing improvised Electro music in metro stations?

My full bio reveals the gloriously questionable journey from respected musician to that guy with too many cables who won't stop talking about filter cutoffs.
